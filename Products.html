Featured Categories

Cleanser

SKIN1004 offers several cleansers, with the most popular being the Madagascar Centella Ampoule Foam. This cleanser is known for its gentle yet effective cleansing power, suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin. It uses Centella Asiatica extract from Madagascar to soothe and hydrate the skin while removing impurities and excess sebum. Another popular option is the Madagascar Centella Light Cleansing Oil, which is a lightweight, oil-based cleanser that effectively removes makeup, dirt, and sunscreen.

Toner

SKIN1004 offers several toner options, each designed with specific skin concerns in mind. The "Madagascar Centella Tea-Trica Purifying Toner" is formulated for oily and acne-prone skin, focusing on exfoliation and sebum control. The "Madagascar Centella Hyalu-Cica Brightening Toner" aims to hydrate, exfoliate gently, and brighten the skin with a focus on hydration. The "Madagascar Centella Probio-Cica Essence Toner" is designed for sensitive skin, providing calming and barrier-strengthening benefits through fermented Centella Asiatica and other beneficial ingredients.

Moisturizer

SKIN1004 offers several moisturizers, with the Madagascar Centella Cream and the Madagascar Centella Soothing Cream being popular choices. These creams are formulated with Centella Asiatica extract from Madagascar, known for its soothing and hydrating properties. They help calm and replenish skin damaged by external factors and strengthen the skin's barrier.

Sun Cream

SKIN1004 offers several sunscreens with varying features, including chemical and physical (mineral) options, and some with tone-up and tinted effects. Key ingredients across their sunscreens include Centella Asiatica extract for soothing and calming the skin, and they are generally formulated to be lightweight and suitable for sensitive skin
